## Burn Mechanism

### Burn Sources and Rates

The protocol has a deflationary component through transaction fee burns:

| Burn Source | Burn Rate | Description |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| PoS Gas Fees | 20% | Transaction fees on main chain |
| PoC Anchoring | 15% | Contribution submission fees |
| Sequencer Gas | 25% | Layer 2 transaction fees |
| Smart Contracts | 30% | Contract execution fees |
| AI Queries | 25% | AI inference fees |
| Messaging | 10% | Cross-chain messaging fees |

### Treasury Burn Redirect

**10% of all burns redirected to treasury** instead of being burned
- Provides additional treasury income
- Reduces overall deflationary pressure
- Balances inflation vs deflation

### Expected Burn Impact

**Year 1 Estimate:**
- Transaction volume: 100M transactions
- Average gas: 0.01 OMNI
- Total fees: 1,000,000 OMNI
- Burned (90%): 900,000 OMNI
- Treasury (10%): 100,000 OMNI

**Net Inflation Year 1:**
- Minted: 22,500,000 OMNI
- Burned: 900,000 OMNI
- **Net New Supply:** 21,600,000 OMNI (2.88% effective inflation)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can the 1.5B supply cap be changed?
**A:** No. The supply cap is **immutable** and cannot be changed through governance or any other mechanism.

### Q: What happens when the supply cap is reached?
**A:** Inflation stops completely. The network continues to operate with staking rewards coming from transaction fees only (PoS becomes deflationary via burns).

### Q: Can team members sell immediately after cliff?
**A:** Technically yes, but tokens vest linearly, so only small amounts are unlocked initially. After the 1-year cliff, ~4.17M OMNI unlock per month over 4 years.

### Q: How is circulating supply calculated?
**A:** Circulating = Total Supply - Unvested Team - Unvested Advisors. Treasury and Community Pool are reported separately as they're controlled funds.

### Q: What if inflation is too high?
**A:** Governance can reduce inflation rate through proposals. Minimum is 0.5%, maximum is 5%. Community votes on changes.

### Q: Will there be token burns?
**A:** Yes. 90% of transaction fees are burned (10% goes to treasury). This creates deflationary pressure offsetting inflation.

### Q: When do advisors start receiving tokens?
**A:** December 1, 2025 (6 months after launch). Then linear unlock over 24 months until December 1, 2027.

### Q: When does team start receiving tokens?
**A:** June 1, 2026 (12 months after launch). Then linear unlock over 48 months until June 1, 2030.

### Q: How is treasury spending controlled?
**A:** 3-of-5 multisig for all transactions. Large spends additionally require community governance approval.

### Q: What backs the treasury income?
**A:** 10% of inflation emissions (~2.25M OMNI/year) + 10% of all burns (~100K OMNI/year) + staking rewards if delegated.
